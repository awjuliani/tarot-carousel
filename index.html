About This Work

Meditations on the Tarot: A Journey into Christian Hermeticism is a profound spiritual work written anonymously and published posthumously in 1980. Later revealed to be the work of Valentin Tomberg, the book presents the 22 Major Arcana of the Tarot as symbolic keys to Christian mysticism, Hermeticism, and the perennial philosophy.

Each card serves as a meditation on archetypal spiritual truths, weaving together insights from Catholic mysticism, Kabbalah, alchemy, and depth psychology. The work invites contemplative engagement rather than mere intellectual study, treating the Tarot as a tool for spiritual awakening and inner transformation.
